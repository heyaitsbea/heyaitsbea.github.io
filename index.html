WHO AM I?

When asked to describe myself in one word, my portmanteau-loving brain came up with "communi-creator".

This sums up my two main strengths as a teammate, student, and person. I am someone who is driven by passion, whether it is for brainstorming a new project, solving problems, or helping others succeed.

When choosing my majors, I found the combination of computer science and communications perfect for me because I love exploring the middleground between technology and human experience. I found this middleground through teaching computer science to beginners, working at the school tech help desk, and leading marketing efforts for one of the university's most diverse beginner-friendly hackathons!
I knew I was passionate about technology, and even more passionate about letting everyone else know how great it was.

Outside my studies, I've spent college gaining experience in publicity, multimedia, performing arts, and children's education, further instilling in me that I thrived in collaborative and creative spaces. Throughout in college, I've learned that this love of mine can apply to both the arts and the sciences in so many countless ways.

I ultimately strive to make technology easily accessible, for those of different experience levels, abilities, and disciplines.
