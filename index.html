WHO AM I?

When asked to describe myself in one word, my portmanteau-loving brain came up with "communi-creator".

This sums up my two main strengths as a teammate, student, and person. I am someone who is driven by passion, whether it is for brainstorming a new project or helping others succeed. Best case scenario - I can create something that will be of great use to someone else! I grew up on writing, reading, video-editing, coding, theatre, and music - showing me just how many creative avenues there are. This led me to pursue computer science and new media in college, as well as side hobbies working in student theatre, student television, and student hackathons. I believe that the intersection of art and technology is where some of the greatest innovations come to life - in entertainment, business, sciences, and more, and it is with these innovations that we can help the people of now and tomorrow grow and flourish.
